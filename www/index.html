Ghosts Overboard for Maemo


Ghosts Overboard is a game for maemo5 (in practise Nokia's N900 device). An early release (0.1.0) with just one level is available.

The story & playing

A group of ghots was sailing with their ghost ship when a storm hit them. The ghosts fell overboard and were scattered across the nearby waters. It is now your task to rescue them. The ghost  ship is controlled by tilting the device. Pick up the ghosts and avoid the rocks. If  the ship hits a rock, all ghosts in it will fall overboard again.

Download

You can install Ghosts Overboard on your N900 from the extras-develrepository.  However, as usual for  extras-devel, the game is not fully tested and not necessarily safe. Battery drainage is the most likely problem to occur. It is not recommended to leave it on foreground without being paused after playing for long time periods. Remember to close the extras-devel repository after installing to avoid unwanted updating of your applications to development versions.

Bug reports and feature requests are welcome on the application's bugtracker. Source code with recent development snapshot can be found in the project's  git repository.
