Ghosts Overboard is a game for maemo5 (Nokia N900) and 'Meego 1.2 Harmattan' (Nokia N9 & Nokia N950) . The latest release is versiom 0.4.0 with highscore and another levelset with five levels. Also the vibration effects of the Harmattan version are now working.

The story & playing

A group of ghosts was sailing with their ghost ship when a storm hit them. The ghosts fell overboard and were scattered across the nearby waters. It is now your task to rescue them. The ghost  ship is controlled by tilting the device. Pick up the ghosts and avoid the rocks and octopuses. If  the ship hits a rock or an octopus, all ghosts in it will fall overboard again. Tap the screen to pause and show the menu.
