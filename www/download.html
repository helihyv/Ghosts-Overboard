Download for N900

Ghosts Overboard is available in the extras-devel repository.  However, as usual for  extras-devel, the game is not fully tested and not necessarily safe. Remember to close the extras-devel repository after installing to avoid unwanted updating of your applications to development versions.

Download for N9 and N950

A binary package of Ghosts overboard can be downloaded from the game's entry page in the Meego Coding Competition 2011. Sources of this package can be downloaded here. The packaging files are in the form used by QtCreator.
